Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Colors Strike ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected when you use our app and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

Colors Strike itself does not require you to create an account and does not directly collect personal information. However, third-party services integrated into the app may collect certain data:

2. Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display ads in the app. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is used to serve personalized or contextual advertisements. AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

3. RevenueCat (Purchases)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Purchase history (which products were purchased)
  • Subscription status (active, expired, etc.)

This data is used solely to deliver purchased content and manage subscriptions. It is not shared with third parties for advertising. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. Apple Crash Reports (Diagnostics)

If you opt in to share diagnostics with app developers through your device settings, Apple may share:

  • Crash logs and performance data

This data is anonymized and used solely to identify and fix bugs. You can control this in your device's Settings → Privacy → Analytics.

5. Data Tracking

AdMob may use device identifiers and ad data for tracking purposes as defined by Apple — specifically, linking data collected from this app with third-party data for targeted advertising or advertising measurement.

The app respects Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If prompted, you can choose to allow or deny tracking.

6. Data Not Collected

Colors Strike does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information (unless you voluntarily provide it via our feedback form)
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or other personal files
  • Health, financial, or sensitive information

7. Children's Privacy

Colors Strike is rated 12+ and is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Third-party services (AdMob, RevenueCat) retain data according to their own policies.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. For AdMob data, visit Google's data controls. For any concerns, contact us below.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please reach out through our feedback form.
